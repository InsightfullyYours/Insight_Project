Welcome to Hospital BedPlan


Hospital BedPlan is a hospital scheduling website that uses patient data and scheduled admission date to predict the patient length of stay and optimally schedule the beds. These predictions can be used to more tightly schedule the maternity ward, increasing the throughput of patients and generating revenue for the hospital.
