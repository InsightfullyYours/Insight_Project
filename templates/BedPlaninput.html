Welcome to Hospital BedPlan


Hospital BedPlan is a hospital scheduling website that uses patient data and estimated admission date to predict the patient length of stay and optimally schedule the beds. At this time, it focuses on the maternity ward, defined as all patients seen under the auspices of Obstetrics and Newborns. Its predictions can be used to more tightly schedule the maternity ward, increasing the throughput of patients and generating revenue for the hospital. The predicted length of stay also be used to focus care on high-needs patients.


There is an introductory presentation available to provide more comprehensive details.


This demonstration uses a de-identified Maternity, Obstetrics, and Neonatal ward EMR database to demonstrate the capabilities of the system.


Please select the number of patient records from the EMR system to schedule.
