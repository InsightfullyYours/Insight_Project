Hospital BedPlan Database Details

The Medical Information Mart for Intensive Care-III (MIMIC-III Database) is a large, freely-available database comprising de-identified health-related data associated with over forty thousand patients who stayed in care units of the Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center between 2001 and 2012.

The Database includes demographic as well as inpatient and limited outpatient medical care information. This information covers everything from the initial and final diagnoses, to lab procedures and results, to hour-by-hour chart information. Please see the documentation (linked above) for full details.
